Hatsune Miku


Who is She?

According to Wikipedia, Hatsune Miku (初音ミク), sometimes called Miku Hatsune, is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media and its official mascot character, a 16-year-old girl with long, turquoise twintails. She's code-named CV01 in Vocaloid

3 facts about her:

  1. Her voice is provided by Saki Fujita
  2. She was initially released in August 2007
  3. Hatsune Miku is featured in 2,643 songs , 1,108 albums , 55 series , 69 events, for now...
